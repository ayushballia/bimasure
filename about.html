About us

We are dedicated to providing the best insurance options for our customers. Through our platform we aim to service more than 9 million insurance buyers and over 50 insurers who transact a few lakh crores of insurance cover and a few thousand crores of insurance premium every year. We pride ourselves on providing a secure and reliable platform for our customers to confidently choose the insurance coverage that best meets their needs.

Our founders had one objective: to bring transparency to insurance. They started by simplifying plan information, preventing mis-selling and policy lapses, and reimagining the insurance industry. Today, we continue to prioritize transparency and empower our customers to make informed decisions about their coverage.

We are proud of the changes we have made to the insurance industry, but we know there is more work to do. Our goal is to bring innovative technologies to the field and provide a health and financial safety net for more households in India.
