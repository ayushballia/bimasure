health insurance

A health insurance policy is a contract between a policyholder and an insurance company, in which the insurer provides financial assistance for medical expenses in exchange for a premium. The policyholder can either opt for reimbursement for medical expenses such as hospital and surgical bills or utilize the cashless claim feature. With the cashless claim feature, the policyholder can receive treatment at a network hospital without worrying about payments as the insurance company and the hospital have an association to settle the bills.

To find the best health insurance plan, you can compare multiple policies online. Purchasing health insurance online allows you to select the one that meets your specific requirements.
